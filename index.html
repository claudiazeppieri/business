General Information

Looking to buy a cheap phone? Are you wanting to help the environment? Well, we are here to help! GreenTech specializes in providing recyclable goods for those who either cannot afford to purchase a brand new technological product, or for those who simply want to help the environment. We have an immense amount of product, provided to us from people around the community and from various tech giants. Our primary goal is to reduce the Earth's overall carbon footprint. Despite this being a small company, we aim to spread awareness in helping the environment. You can either purchase a used phone, computer, tablet, etc, or you can sell your own to us. Help us make the world a better place, even carrying out minimal acts such as this contributes to a much greater cause!
